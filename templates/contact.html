Frequently Asked Questions

What is PneumoScan?

PneumoScan is an AI-powered tool that analyzes chest X-ray images to detect pneumonia with high accuracy using the VGG16 model. It assists medical professionals in making faster and more accurate diagnoses.

How accurate is the model?

The VGG16 model achieves over 92% accuracy on the test dataset, validated against the Chest X-ray Pneumonia Dataset from Kaggle.

Can I contribute?

Yes, contributions are welcome! Please use the contact form above to reach out, and we'll guide you on how to get involved in our project.
